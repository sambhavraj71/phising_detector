Security Tips & Best Practices

Check Website Indicators

  • Always look for HTTPS in the URL
  • Check for padlock icon in address bar
  • Verify domain spelling carefully
  • Avoid sites with too many pop-ups
  • Check for professional design quality

Common Phishing Signs

  • Urgent requests for personal info
  • Generic greetings ("Dear User")
  • Poor grammar and spelling errors
  • Suspicious email attachments
  • Too-good-to-be-true offers

Protection Measures

  • Use two-factor authentication
  • Keep software updated regularly
  • Use strong, unique passwords
  • Enable spam filters
  • Regular security awareness training
